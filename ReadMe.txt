PRESS ESCAPE TO OPEN/CLOSE THE MENU (AND UNLOCK/LOCK THE MOUSE)

WASD Movement (q/e up down)

Menu:
Hit PLAY to start
Note* the targets hit counter
Sensitivity Slider
Two Gamemodes -> Click and Hover

Click Mode:
Simply click at the red targets to get the point
Targets change locations every 10 seconds and move slowly in one direction

Hover Mode:
Hover over a red target for 5 seconds to get the point
Targets change location every 15 seconds and move slowly in one direction
Targets are given more leeway with a larger radius to make it easier
